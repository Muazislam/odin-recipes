Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian casserole featuring layers of wide, flat pasta stacked with a rich, savory meat sauce, creamy cheeses, and herbs. Baked until bubbling and golden-brown, each hearty bite offers the perfect balance of tender noodles, robust tomato flavor, and gooey, melted cheese.

The Layers of Flavor

A well-crafted lasagna is a true "labor of love" defined by its harmonious layers:

  • The Pasta: Wide, ribbon-edged pasta sheets boiled perfectly al dente or layered directly as oven-ready (no-boil) noodles to absorb the surrounding sauces.
  • The Meat Sauce (Ragù): A slow-simmered blend of ground beef, Italian sausage, crushed tomatoes, garlic, and aromatic herbs.
  • The Creamy Cheese: A lush blend of ricotta, whole-milk mozzarella, and freshly grated Parmesan that melts into the pasta, providing richness.
  • The Béchamel: In traditional Italian or Bolognese-style lasagnas, this silky white sauce is used instead of ricotta to bind the layers.

Serving Suggestions

Because lasagna is incredibly rich and filling, it is best served with light, acidic sides that cut through the richness. It pairs beautifully with a crisp, tangy garden salad, warm garlic bread, or roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

The Pasta

  • Lasagna noodles: 12 to 15 sheets (standard or oven-ready).

The Meat Sauce

  • Ground meat: 1 pound lean ground beef or mild Italian sausage.
  • Aromatics: 1 medium yellow onion and 3 minced garlic cloves.
  • Tomatoes: 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes and 2 tablespoons tomato paste.
  • Herbs: 1 tablespoon dried Italian seasoning (or fresh basil and oregano).
  • Seasoning: Salt, black pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes.

The Cheese Filling

  • Ricotta cheese: 15 ounces (or cottage cheese as a lighter substitute).
  • Egg: 1 large egg to bind the cheese mixture together.
  • Parmesan: 1⁄2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh herb: 1⁄4 cup chopped fresh parsley.

How to make Lasagna

While homemade lasagna may have a few steps, each step is easy – and I assure you it’s worth the time; the perfect Italian meal!

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
